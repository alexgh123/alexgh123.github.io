I don’t think the “Explain how to use affirmation to help you succeed” learning competency was really directly addressed. I will explain it here: Basically, people will refuse to accept information, input or interactions that threaten their self-esteem (more specifically, the integrity of their self image). When people are presented with information that threatens their self esteem, they do not accept the information. However, if you can remind them of their self esteem in an unrelated area of their life, such as a volunteer organization, they will be more open to the information that might make them feel bad about themselves.
